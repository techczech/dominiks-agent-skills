# Summary Card Template

Use this template for creating shareable summaries for external use (social media, blogs, personal knowledge bases).

---

## YAML Header Template

```yaml
---
type: extract
extract_type: summary-card
paper_key: {ZOTERO_KEY}
paper_title: "{FULL_TITLE}"
extracted_date: "{YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ}"
tags:
  - "{topic-tag}"
  - "{method-tag}"
  - "summary"
links:
  - "[[{folder-name}/00-source]]"
  - "[[{folder-name}/reviews/gelman-review]]"
  - "[[{folder-name}/reviews/quick-read]]"
---
```

Replace all `{PLACEHOLDERS}` with actual values.

## Format Notes

**Length**: Aim for 200-300 words total
**Tone**: Accessible but informed
**Audience**: Educated readers who aren't specialists
**Purpose**: Quick understanding and sharing

**Platforms**:
- Twitter/X threads
- LinkedIn posts
- Personal blogs
- Research newsletters
- Note-taking apps (Notion, Obsidian, etc.)
